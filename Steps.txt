Step 1: If you are using the AWS Organizations feature, ensure the following services are enabled:
    https://us-east-1.console.aws.amazon.com/organizations/v2/home/services/CloudTrail
    https://us-east-1.console.aws.amazon.com/organizations/v2/home/services/CloudFormation%20StackSets
Step 2: Create a CloudFormation Stack (Ensure you are in your preferred AWS region) using Cyngular's template https://us-east-1.console.aws.amazon.com/cloudformation/home?region=us-east-1#/stacks/create/review?templateURL=https://cyngular-onboarding-templates.s3.amazonaws.com/v3/stacks/CyngularOnBoarding.yaml&stackName=CyngularOnBoarding
Step 3: After the deployment is complete, enter your management account ID and select your preferred regions. Then, click "Connect Cloud Provider"
